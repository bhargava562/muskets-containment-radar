- Shows real-time impact of containment

## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM

**Color Palette:**
- **Cyan (#06B6D4)** - Action, recommended paths, secured funds
- **Red (#EF4444)** - Critical alerts, mule accounts, harsh actions
- **Amber (#F59E0B)** - Warnings, compromised accounts, medium priority
- **Emerald (#10B981)** - Innocent merchants, success states, low risk
- **Slate (#1E293B)** - Base UI, glassmorphism panels

**Typography:**
- **Numbers:** Bold, large, gradient effects
- **Metrics:** Mono font for technical precision
- **Impacts:** Regular weight, readable, contextual

**Animations:**
- **Layout transitions:** Smooth item removal/addition
- **Pulse effects:** Urgency indicators, recommended actions
- **Stagger animations:** Timeline, AI summary bullets
- **Scale on interaction:** Buttons feel satisfying to click

**Glassmorphism:**
- `glass-panel` - Standard panels
- `glass-panel-dark` - Darker variant for nested content
- Backdrop blur + border + subtle gradient
